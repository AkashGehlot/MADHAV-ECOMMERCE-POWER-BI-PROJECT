# MADHAV-ECOMMERCE-POWER-BI-PROJECT
The ecommerce business is performing well with steady sales and profit. 
Strong performance in Maharashtra and high demand for clothing products show a clear growth direction.
Cash on Delivery remains the preferred payment method, but increasing digital payments can improve efficiency. 
Mid-year profit drops highlight the need for better seasonal planning. 
Overall, the business has strong potential for sustainable growth with focused improvements in weaker areas.
